OUR GOALS

Our goals are to reduce waste, save energy, and promote sustainable habits throughout our school. We aim to increase recycling, encourage environmentally friendly choices, and raise awareness about protecting our planet. By working together, we hope to create lasting changes and make our school a positive example for others to follow.
