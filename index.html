WHO WE ARE

We are a group of dedicated students working together to make a positive difference in our school and community. We care about the environment and believe that small actions can lead to big change. By sharing ideas, taking action, and supporting one another, we are building a greener, more sustainable future for everyone. We are The Eco School AG. A club that forms during Monday's lunch period to help our school become more environmentally friendly. Together with our teacher Mr. Kiely, we help our school improve the planet.
